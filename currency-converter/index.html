Clothing Size Converter

Instantly convert clothing sizes between international sizing systems. Supports Women's & Men's categories across 10 regional standards.

Privacy

No data collection. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. No accounts are required.

No tracking. Zero analytics, advertising, fingerprinting, or third-party SDKs. No network requests are made.

Local only. All conversions run entirely on your device. Your preferences (theme, default countries) are stored in your browser's local storage and never leave your device.

No permissions. This app requires no special permissions — no location, camera, contacts, or notifications.

Your rights (GDPR). Since no personal data is collected, there is nothing to access, export, or delete. You can clear local storage in your browser settings at any time to reset all preferences.

How to Use
1. Choose a Category

Tap the category name at the top (e.g. "Dress") to pick what you want to convert — dresses, shoes, jeans, bras, and more.

2. Set Your Regions

Tap a region code (e.g. "CA/US" or "UK") to change which country you're converting from or to. Tap the swap arrows to quickly flip them.

3. Enter Your Size

Use the keypad to type your size number. The converted size appears automatically above. For shoes, use the decimal key for half sizes (e.g. 7.5).

4. Letter Sizes

For categories like Dress, Top, or Jacket, tap the "Size" button below the keypad to pick a letter size (S, M, L, XL, etc.) instead of typing a number.

5. Bra Sizes

For bras, enter the band size on the keypad and use the "Cup" button to select your cup size (A, B, C, etc.). The cup letter carries over to the converted size.

Tips

Long-press the backspace key to clear all input. Conversions are approximate — always check the brand's own size chart for the best fit.
